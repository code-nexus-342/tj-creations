    What services does TJ Creations offer?

    We offer a full range of interior design services, from concept to completion. This includes space planning, furniture selection, window treatments, lighting, and more

    Our fees vary depending on the scope of the project. We offer a free consultation to discuss your needs and provide a custom quote.

    What is your design style?

    We specialize in creating elegant and sophisticated interiors that reflect your personal style. We can work with a variety of styles, from traditional to contemporary.

    How long does it take to complete an interior design project?

    The timeline for each project varies depending on the scope and complexity. We will work with you to create a timeline that meets your needs.

    Yes, we offer a full range of event design services, from floral arrangements to lighting and decor.
